【本の内容】
This book assumes two things: Women of color have achieved a lot in the legal profession in the U.S. but still have a long way to go; and women of color in the legal profession want to do good work and succeed. An African American lawyer Parker provides practical advice and strategies for all sorts of situations from managing relationships on the job and with clients to deciding when it"s time to move on. The author also interviewed dozens of successful women lawyers while writing this book and their stories and tips are a substantial part of each chapter. While designed for young women of color associates this book will be helpful for any woman who practices law. Annotation ?2011 Book News Inc. Portland OR (booknews.com)
