【本の内容】
A fully revised and updated guide to each stage of building a structure--from the ground up
This comprehensive step-by-step guide provides a complete overview of carpentry and construction for every aspect of the construction process--from worksite foundations and structural systems to interior work and finishes.
Carpentry Andamp; Construction Fifth Edition
takes you through each phase of home construction in such detail that you can use it to build a home. Clear instructions along with 1500 photographs and illustrations show every step of the construction process--from selecting tools to pouring the foundation to finishing the interior. The book includes illustrated instructions for such projects as kitchen and bathroom remodeling installing solar heating building skylights constructing fences decks and patios and more. Information on the latest sustainable construction options is included.
