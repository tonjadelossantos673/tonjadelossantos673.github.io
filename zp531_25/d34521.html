Please note:
1. The wireless Bluetooth connection distance between mic and phone should be within 10 meters, without obstacles.
2. Bluetooth has memory function, the phone connected before will auto reconnect to the mic that has entered bluetooth mode. So, if you want to connect your mic to the other phone, please turn off Bluetooth of the previous phone that is within the mic’s Bluetooth range.
3. Duet function requires 2 mics, please enjoy duet fun by buying 2 mics. In duet mode, the distance between 2 mics should not exceed 6m (better to be within 4 m) without obstacles. After turning on duet button, please wait about 4s to let both mics search and connect each other.
4. This is a professional unidirectional microphone that only picks up sound from the top of the mic, none from side. Please talk to the middle of mic top to get pure and clear target sound source without any ambient noise.
5. When you use it as a speaker for listening to music, please turn off MIC volume.
