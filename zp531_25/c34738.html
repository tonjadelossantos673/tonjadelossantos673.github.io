Here are some important tips to keep in mind:

1. Before using the vacuum, make sure to fully charge it to ensure optimal suction power.

2. When charging the handheld vacuum, remember to turn it off. Avoid using the vacuum while it"s charging to preserve the battery life.

3. Regularly clean the filter net to maintain maximum dust collection efficiency.

4. The filter net can be washed. After washing, please allow it to dry thoroughly.

5. To prevent liquid from reaching the motor, avoid holding the mini vacuum upside-down or laying it on its side after using it to vacuum liquids. Remember to empty the dust cup promptly.
