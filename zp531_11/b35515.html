商品コメント SUPER CHALLENGER MAKES KIDS SMARTER and PARENTS CAN ACTUALLY SEE IT HAPPENING, because the numbers and letters on the puzzle patterns show the level that a child has achieved as a child plays with it. It’s a GROWTH CHART on 3 different levels. The ascending scale of letters and numbers are like a measuring stick. They show a child gaining greater and greater logical thinking skills each time a child plays with it.
SUPER CHALLENGER expands the MightyMind series by providing 3 different advanced levels of puzzle groups with double the number of design tiles so 2 CHILDREN CAN PLAY TOGETHER. Yet, it’s nonーcompetitive, so Children playing with MightyMind Super Challenger progress at their own pace without feeling any competitive pressure.
The entire series leads children step by step through pathways that gradually become AMAZINGLY COMPLEX. Kids get “glued” to it, because it generates a feeling of success and it BUILDS A CHILD"S CONFIDENCE AND SELFーESTEEM. The Super Challenger series is an extension of basic MightyMind which originated way back in 1978, based on B.F. Skinner"s (the Harvard behaviorist) development of linear programming.
Super Challenger contains MightyMind Challenger puzzle patterns at a beginner level. It advances to MightyMind Sea Explorer that contains an array of colorful aquatic themes. The most challenging level of play are the double size BIG IDEA BOOK puzzle patterns. These require both sets of the included (64) design tiles.
IT’S MORE THAN JUST A TOY. Kids playing with MightyMind SuperMind Challenger, develop a solid foundation that becomes a platform for building more advanced complex essential skills like language, math and problem solving. It develops a base for lifelong thinking skills that precede coding, STEM and STEAM.
