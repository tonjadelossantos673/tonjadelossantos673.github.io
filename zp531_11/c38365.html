【本の内容】
This is a superbly illustrated volume showing the very latest trends in architecture and design for small bars. The bar is a ubiquitous space in modern civilization - from traditional English pubs to modern European nightspots and from French wine bars to the Hookah bars of North Africa and the Middle East - every city will have its own take on the bar. But the bar is not only a place where people come together it is also a melting pot for ideas imagination and creativity - especially in the world of architecture and design. This superbly illustrated volume brings together a number of projects that showcase the very latest in contemporary design and architecture for small bars.
