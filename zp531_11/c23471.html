　Lumbar support for motorcycle riders (ergonomic backrest)

・Why uses a lumbar support？
Our vertebral column， or spine， has a natural forward curvature， which creates the hollow shape of our lower back， or lumbar region． The abdominal muscles give the lumbar spine support， but if these muscles become slack or tired， the spine starts to acquire a backward curvature instead． If you remain in this posture for a prolonged period， the muscles shorten， which causes them to tense up with the result that you get discomfort
in your lower back．

・What is a lumbar support？
Effective lumbar support promotes healthy sitting posture． It specifically supports the lower back and adapts to your body shape． Virtually all car makers equips the front seats of their vehicles with lumbar supports to help combat back pain．

・Ideal for motorcycle riders！
Every biker knows what it’s like when you finally stop at a traffic light after riding uninterrupted for hours． The first thing you do is to press your fist against the small of your back to relieve the tension． So a lumbar support needs to be a close fit but without pressing． The new lumbar support is made from gel so that it adapts to your particular body shape without any pressure． Its shape supports the natural curvature of your lower back and ensures that the muscles in this region are always relaxed． This prevents the back pain and tenseness that are all too familiar after hours in the saddle．
