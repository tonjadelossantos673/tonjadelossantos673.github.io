Super7 - Bootsy Collins ReAction Figure

Don’t fake the funk!
This 3.75” articulated Bootsy Collins ReAction Figure features the legendary bassist
in his starry red & white jumpsuit and sunglasses, and includes with his signature star bass guitar.
Packaged on a cardback with foil accents, you can cut out your own pair of Bootsy glasses from the back!
If the groove is in your heart, there’s no way you’ll pass on adding the red & white Bootsy Collins ReAction Figure to your collection!
